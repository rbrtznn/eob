TRANSLATION NOTE FOR ROMANS

In this complex theological masterpiece, St. Paul often used the Greek word ga.r to indicate a certain logical continuity. This word is typically rendered by ‘for’ in traditional translations, but such a consistent approach quickly becomes awkward and burdensome. For this reason, the EOB uses a variety of English equivalents for ga.r, such as ‘indeed,’ ‘as it is,’ ‘certainly,’ ‘for,’ etc.
